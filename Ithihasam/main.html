I SEE ONLY ROUND BLACK EYE OF THE BIRD

-By Valmiki

When Dronacharya was teaching archery to is students,he once asked them to shoot a bird on the tree by aiming its eye

He called them one by one and showing the bird,he asked them what they saw

Everyone except arjuna said that they saw the tree mthe branchesmthe bird etc and so every one missed the target

Lord Krishna himself declares in bhagavad gite verse 2.41:

vyavasaayaatmikaa buddhir /ekeha kuru -nandana bahu-shaakhaa hy anataash ca/byddhayo 'vyavasaayinaam

"Those who are on tis path are resolute in purpose,and their aim is one ,O beloved childs of the kurus,the intelligence of those who are irresolute is many-branched "

Arjuna had been practising archery day and night and was the best archer among the students of Dronacarya

So When aujuna's turn came drona inquired him as follows.

Drona:"What do you see?"
Arjuna:" I see the eye of the bird"
Drona:"Do You see the tree"
Arjuna:"No"
Drona:"Do you see the branch ?"
Arjuna:"No"
Drona:"Do you see the bird"
Arjuna:"No"
Drona:"Then what else do you see,Arjuna?"
Arjuna:"Nohting"

Saying so arjuna released the arrow and ith just hit the target straight

Moral of the Story

"Thus when ones's consciousness is completely fixed on the absolute truth the supreme personality of godhead,one no longer sees duality ,or internal and external reality the example is given of the arrow maker who was so absorbed in making a straight arrow that he did not even see or notice the king himself,who was passing right next to him"
